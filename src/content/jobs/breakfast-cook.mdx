## Position Overview

As a Breakfast Cook at Valiant Vineyards Bed & Breakfast, you will prepare fresh, hearty breakfasts for our overnight guests each morning. This is a hands-on kitchen role where you'll take pride in starting each guest's day with a memorable meal made from quality ingredients.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare and cook breakfast menu items including eggs, omelets, pancakes, waffles, bacon, hash browns, and more
- Set up and stock cooking stations before service begins
- Ensure food is cooked according to recipes and customer preferences
- Maintain cleanliness and organization of the kitchen and workstations
- Follow food safety and sanitation guidelines
- Monitor food inventory and inform management of shortages
- Plate meals attractively and ensure proper portion sizes
- Assist with prep work for other meals when needed
- Work efficiently during busy breakfast hours

## Qualifications

- Basic cooking and food preparation skills
- Reliable transportation to the winery

## Work Environment

You'll work in the Bed & Breakfast kitchen, preparing meals in a cozy, home-style setting. Early morning hours are required, with shifts typically ending by late morning. Part-time positions are available.
