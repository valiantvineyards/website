## Position Overview

As a Breakfast Cook at Valiant Vineyards Bed & Breakfast, you will prepare fresh, hearty breakfasts for our overnight guests each morning. This is a hands-on kitchen role where you'll take pride in starting each guest's day with a memorable meal made from quality ingredients.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare and cook breakfast items including eggs, pancakes, waffles, bacon, sausage, and seasonal specialties
- Set up the breakfast service area and ensure an attractive presentation
- Follow recipes and maintain consistent food quality and portion sizes
- Maintain a clean, sanitary kitchen in compliance with food safety standards
- Wash dishes, utensils, and kitchen equipment
- Monitor and manage food inventory; communicate supply needs to management
- Accommodate dietary restrictions and food allergies when notified
- Break down and clean the kitchen and dining area after breakfast service
- Assist with food preparation for special events as needed

## Qualifications

- Previous cooking or food preparation experience preferred
- Basic knowledge of food safety and kitchen sanitation practices
- Ability to follow recipes and adapt to guest dietary needs
- Early morning availability required (typical shift begins at 6:00 AM)
- Must be able to stand for extended periods and lift up to 25 lbs
- Strong attention to detail and time management skills
- Team player with a positive attitude
- Reliable transportation to the winery

## Work Environment

You'll work in the Bed & Breakfast kitchen, preparing meals in a cozy, home-style setting. Early morning hours are required, with shifts typically ending by late morning. The kitchen can be warm, and the role involves standing, bending, and lifting throughout your shift.
