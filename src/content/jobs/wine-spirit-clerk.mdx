## Position Overview

As a Wine/Spirit Clerk at Valiant Vineyards, you will be the face of our winery, greeting guests and guiding them through tastings of our award-winning wines and handcrafted spirits. This is a customer-focused role perfect for someone who enjoys sharing knowledge, creating memorable experiences, and working in a beautiful vineyard setting.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct wine and spirit tastings for guests, providing knowledgeable descriptions of each product
- Operate the point-of-sale system and process transactions accurately
- Maintain a clean, organized, and inviting tasting room environment
- Stock shelves, displays, and coolers with wine, spirits, and merchandise
- Assist with inventory management and product rotation
- Answer guest questions about our wines, spirits, vineyard, and local area
- Help set up and break down for special events and private tastings
- Follow all state and federal regulations regarding alcohol service
- Encourage wine club memberships and newsletter sign-ups

## Qualifications

- Must be at least 21 years of age
- Previous retail, hospitality, or customer service experience preferred
- Knowledge of wine and/or spirits is a plus but not required — we'll train the right person
- Friendly, outgoing personality with excellent communication skills
- Ability to stand for extended periods and lift up to 30 lbs (cases of wine)
- Must be available to work weekends and some holidays
- Reliable transportation to the winery

## Work Environment

The tasting room is a welcoming, social environment where you'll interact with guests throughout your shift. The role involves standing, walking, and occasionally lifting cases of wine or spirits. Weekend availability is essential as these are our busiest days.
