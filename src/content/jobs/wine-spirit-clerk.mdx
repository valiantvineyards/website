## Position Overview

As a Wine/Spirit Clerk at Valiant Vineyards, you will be the face of our winery, assisting customers with wine and spirit selection and ensuring excellent customer service. This role requires knowledge of wine varieties, regions, and food pairings to help customers make informed purchasing decisions.

## Responsibilities

- Assist customers in selecting wines or spirits based on taste preferences, occasion, and budget
- Provide recommendations on wine and spirit regions, grape varieties, and food pairings
- Stock and organize wine and spirit shelves, displays, and storage areas
- Monitor inventory levels and report low stock to management
- Ensure proper pricing, labeling, and signage for wine and spirit products
- Rotate stock and maintain product freshness (first-in, first-out method)
- Maintain cleanliness and organization in the wine and spirit sections
- Process customer purchases using the point-of-sale (POS) system
- Verify customer age and comply with alcohol sales laws and regulations
- Assist with wine and spirit tastings, promotions, and special events when scheduled
- Answer the winery phone and direct calls or take messages

## Qualifications

- Must be at least 21 years of age
- High school diploma or equivalent preferred
- Basic knowledge of wine varieties, regions, and terminology preferred
- Previous retail or customer service experience is a plus
- Strong communication and customer service skills
- Ability to lift and carry boxes (typically up to 40–50 lbs)
- Ability to stand for extended periods and work flexible hours

## Preferred Skills

- Passion for wine, spirits, and beverage culture
- Wine or spirit certification (such as introductory sommelier or wine education courses) is a plus
- Ability to learn quickly about new wine and spirit products and trends

## Work Environment

The tasting room is a welcoming, social environment where you'll interact with customers and team members throughout your shift. Shifts may include evenings, weekends, and holidays. Part-time positions are available.
