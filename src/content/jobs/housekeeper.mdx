## Position Overview

As a Housekeeper at Valiant Vineyards Bed & Breakfast, you will ensure our guest rooms and common areas are spotlessly clean and beautifully presented. Your attention to detail will help create a welcoming, comfortable experience for every guest who stays with us.

## Responsibilities

- Clean and prepare guest rooms between stays, including making beds, vacuuming, dusting, and sanitizing bathrooms
- Maintain cleanliness of common areas, hallways, and outdoor spaces
- Launder and fold linens, towels, and other bedding materials
- Restock guest amenities such as toiletries, towels, and supplies
- Report any maintenance issues, damage, or repair needs to management
- Follow cleaning checklists to ensure consistent quality standards
- Handle cleaning supplies and equipment safely and responsibly
- Assist with special setup or decorations for events and guest requests
- Manage linen inventory and communicate supply needs

## Qualifications

- Previous housekeeping or cleaning experience preferred but not required
- Strong attention to detail and high standards for cleanliness
- Ability to work independently and manage time effectively
- Must be able to stand, bend, and lift up to 25 lbs throughout shift
- Reliable and punctual — our guests count on rooms being ready on time
- Respectful of guest privacy and property
- Flexible schedule with weekend and holiday availability
- Reliable transportation to the winery

## Work Environment

You'll work throughout the Bed & Breakfast property, including guest rooms, bathrooms, common areas, and laundry facilities. The role is physical, involving bending, lifting, and being on your feet throughout your shift. You'll often work independently but are part of a small, supportive team.
