## Position Overview

As a Housekeeper at Valiant Vineyards Bed & Breakfast, you will ensure our guest rooms and common areas are spotlessly clean and beautifully presented. Your attention to detail will help create a welcoming, comfortable experience for every guest who stays with us.

## Responsibilities

- Clean and sanitize guest rooms, bathrooms, hallways, and common areas
- Make beds and change linens and towels
- Vacuum carpets, sweep and mop floors
- Dust furniture, fixtures, and surfaces
- Restock supplies such as toiletries, towels, and cleaning products
- Empty trash and dispose of waste properly
- Report maintenance issues or damaged items to management
- Follow safety and sanitation standards
- Ensure rooms meet cleanliness standards before guest use
- Wash, fold, and stack guest linens and towels and store in the Bed & Breakfast linen closet

## Required Skills & Qualifications

- Basic knowledge of cleaning methods and equipment
- Attention to detail and organization skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team
- Good time management
- Physical stamina to stand, bend, lift, and walk for long periods

## Preferred Qualifications

- Previous housekeeping or cleaning experience
- Knowledge of cleaning chemicals and safety procedures
- Customer service skills, especially in hotels or hospitality

## Work Environment

You'll work throughout the Bed & Breakfast property, including guest rooms, bathrooms, common areas, and laundry facilities. The role involves standing, bending, lifting, and repetitive tasks. Shifts may include mornings, evenings, weekends, or holidays. Part-time positions are available.
