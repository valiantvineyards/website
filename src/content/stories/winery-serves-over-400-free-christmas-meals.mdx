**Thank You for Making Our Christmas Day Event a Success!**  

On Christmas Day, we had the incredible opportunity to serve 427 meals to members of our community in need. This would not have been possible without the support of our amazing volunteers and the generosity of everyone who participated.

We are deeply grateful to all who helped make this event a success. Your kindness and compassion embody the true spirit of the season.

**Special thanks to:**  
Hy-Vee Vermillion, First Dakota Vermillion, Walker Construction, Dakota Hospital Foundation, Merchant State Bank, Voyage Federal Credit Union, Gregoire Excavation, Vermillion Ace Hardware, Walmart Vermillion, DFW Insurance, Max & Patricia Rosdail, and Volunteers!
