**Saturday, November 22, 2025**  
**11 AM - 3 PM**

Celebrate the season with us at Valiant Vineyards & Stone Faces Distillery during our annual Winter Holiday Market.

Immerse yourself in the holiday spirit as you explore an array of local vendors, seasonal tastings, and festive experiences that capture the joy of the season. From handcrafted gifts to holiday libations, it's a perfect way to spend a winter afternoon.

**Event Highlights**

- Wine and spirit tastings from Valiant Vineyards and Stone Faces Distillery
- Seasonal cocktails and festive pairings
- Local artisan vendors offering unique holiday gifts
- Door prizes throughout the day
- Community Giving: We'll be collecting donations for the local food pantry during the event. Guests are encouraged to bring canned goods, non-perishable food items, or cash donations to help local families this holiday season.
 
Make the season memorable by joining us for a day filled with warmth, laughter, and the spirit of celebration.

Vendors: To register for a spot, please email wine@valiantvineyards.us.
