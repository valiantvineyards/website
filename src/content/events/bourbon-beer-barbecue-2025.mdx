**Date:** Saturday, February 22nd, 2025  
**Location:** Valiant Vineyards Winery & Distillery  
**Tickets:** $80 per person for bourbon/beer/barbecue. $35 for barbecue only

**This incredible evening features:**

- Over 50 bourbons and ryes to taste
- Local craft beers and wines
- A one-of-a-kind handmade bourbon cigar experience crafted live by Wes Teal, aka “Stogie,” a Certified Consumer Tobacconist and [East River Cigar Company](https://eastrivercigar.biz/) co-owner. Witness the art of cigar rolling and take home Clay Turner Bourbon-infused cigars, soaked for 30 days in this signature bourbon!
